Orders & Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and same-day delivery is available in select metropolitan areas for orders placed before 2 PM.

You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships. During peak seasons, delivery times may be slightly longer.

Yes, you can track your order through your ShopHub account. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also track your order by:

  • Logging into your account and visiting "My Orders"
  • Using the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contacting our customer support team

Currently, we ship to all major cities in India. We're working on expanding our international shipping capabilities. For international orders, additional customs duties and taxes may apply, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹999. For orders below ₹999, standard shipping costs ₹99. Express shipping is available for ₹199, and same-day delivery costs ₹499 (where available).

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached and in the original packaging. Some items like intimate apparel, personalized products, and perishable goods are not eligible for return.

To initiate a return:

  1. Log into your ShopHub account
  2. Go to "My Orders" and select the item you want to return
  3. Click "Return Item" and follow the instructions
  4. Print the return label and attach it to your package
  5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your payment method and bank processing times:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • UPI: 2-3 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • Wallet: Instant to 24 hours
